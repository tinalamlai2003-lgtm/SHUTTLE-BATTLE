    How to Play

    You pilot the last operational shuttle in the Call Fleet. Survive incoming waves, collect points, and push deeper into enemy-controlled space.

    • Use the movement keys to dodge incoming ships and projectiles.
    • Fire continuously to clear paths through enemy formations.
    • Higher difficulties increase enemy speed and fire rate.

    Missions are short and focused. Perfect for quick sessions while still leaving room for mastery.
